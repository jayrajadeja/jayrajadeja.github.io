Why SQLite and markdown remain the most resilient storage layer for engineer-focused knowledge management systems in 2024.

## Local-First Philosophy

In an era of cloud dependencies, there is profound value in owning your data completely. A local-first PKM ensures your knowledge graph survives any service shutdown.

## The SQLite Backbone

SQLite provides ACID compliance, full-text search, and zero configuration. It is the most deployed database engine in the world for good reason.

## Markdown as Universal Format

Markdown is the plaintext lingua franca. It renders everywhere, versions cleanly with git, and will be readable in 50 years. No proprietary lock-in, no migration headaches.
